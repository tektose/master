Our mission is to empower people with skills and technologies to uplift their communities.
We run a computer programming bootcamp with the goal of building a community of innovators who will get the opportunity to work with TekTose to engineer technologies to solve socio-economic challenges in our communities.
Learn more about the bootcamp.
TekTose bootcamp is a free intensive 4-week program in Harare Zimbabwe, introducing high schoolers to programming and algorithms. The program ran previously in 2018 and will run in 2021.

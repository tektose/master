Our mission is to empower people with skills and technologies to uplift their communities.
We run a computer programming bootcamp and an essay writing contest with the goal of building a community of innovators who will get the opportunity to work with TekTose to engineer technologies to solve socio-economic challenges in our communities.
Learn more and Join Us.

TekTose bootcamp is a free intensive one week program in Harare, Zimbabwe, introducing high schoolers to programming and algorithms. The program previously ran in 2018 and will run again in 2021.
